PURPOSE

LOCALS seeks to bring attention to small businesses that offer quality services and products while, at the same time, guaranteeing we create the best possible experience at Dominan Republic ‘s Zona Colonial for all its visitors. We do this by sharing with you all the insider details about local gems tourists wouldn’t normally get to know about but are special to the people in the area. By using our knowledge of this part of the city we created a route and made a list of recommended spots for you to pick and choose from depending on what you’re looking for. Our goal is that by sharing these with you we will help you have the most genuinely dominican experience in the popular Zona Colonial, making you feel as if you were just another local. Each of the spots have been carefully chosen for offering something special we think you can’t leave without trying. This way we help small business grow by helping people have a good time. Great combination right? You can visit all these spots while you party aboard our train or if you wanna go solo you can always check the spots to selected and how to get there through our app.
